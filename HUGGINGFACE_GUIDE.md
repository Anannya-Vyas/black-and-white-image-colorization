# 🛡️ NO-CARD DEPLOYMENT: Hugging Face Guide

This is the **"Card-Free"** way to get your website live. It takes 1 minute.

### Step 1: Create a Free Hugging Face Account
1. Go to **[huggingface.co/join](https://huggingface.co/join)**.
2. Sign up with your email (No credit card ever).

### Step 2: Create a New Space
1. Go to **[huggingface.co/new-space](https://huggingface.co/new-space)**.
2. **Name**: `ai-colorizer` (or anything you like).
3. **License**: `mit`.
4. **Space SDK**: Select **`Docker`** (Crucial!).
5. **Docker Template**: Select **`Blank`**.
6. **Public/Private**: Keep it **`Public`**.
7. Click **"Create Space"**.

### Step 3: Connect Your GitHub
1. On the next screen, scroll down to find the **"Connected to GitHub"** section (or look for the "Settings" tab).
2. Connect your GitHub account and select your **`Black-and-white-image-colorization`** repository.
3. Click **"Save"**.

---

### 🎉 That's it!
- Hugging Face will start "Building" your app. 
- Because I pre-configured the model download, it will be much faster.
- Once it's "Running," your app will be live at a link like: `https://huggingface.co/spaces/YOUR_NAME/ai-colorizer`

**You now have a 16GB RAM super-powered AI server for free!**
